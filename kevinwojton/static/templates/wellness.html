Health & Wellness

Wellness for all ages

Cleveland Rocks is dedicated to providing a family-friendly atmosphere and work to cultivate a culture of living well. Between the rock climbing, weightlifting, and courses we will have an option for all styles of workouts. Our goal is to make provide a community and make exercising fun. Quality instructors will be available for more structured work outs. All the amenities of the center will be available for a single monthly cost. If you want to come in for a one time, visit day passes will be available as well.

Ohio City has already begun it's active lifestyle movement. There are a few green spaces and a yoga studio in the area. We plan to buttress the amenities already available in the area and help make our center a key center for an active-lifestyle in Cleveland. We plan to make a world class center and provide a community that espouses the most impactful programming possible.

Promote a Healthy Lifestyle

Cleveland Rocks will cultivate a culture that starts with individuals and ends with the community. We understand that the equipment and amenities only go so far. The culture and community is what drives a healthy lifestyle. Cleveland Rocks works to bring like-minded individuals under the same roof so that people can meet, interact, and be inspired by each other. Our goal is to bring the community together under one roof.
