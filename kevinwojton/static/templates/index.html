Cleveland Rocks

A world class climbing gym in the Ohio City District, Cleveland, Ohio.


Ohio City, 2018

Get ready, something big is coming.

Cleveland Rocks is rehabilitating the historic Franklin Masonic Center in Ohio City. The historical Franklin Masonic Center was built in 1926 and until recently was used by the Masons. It is one of the largest climbing gyms in Ohio and offers additional amenities including a 24-hour workout gym, yoga studio, workout classes and other health/wellness resources. Other activities in the building will be a technology education center and a non-profit dedicated to providing as much impact to the immediate area as possible. Our goal is singular, to make a safe, fun place for active like-minded people to find community.

  • Cleveland Rocks is a world-class climbing facility with 18,000 square feet of climbing, a glass skyroof, and some of the tallest climbing walls in Ohio. The gym will be complemented copious natural lighting and state of the art equipment.
  • The climbing walls will be built for participants of all skill levels. No matter if you are a first-time climber or have been climbing for a decade there will be more than enough climbing for everyone. The climbing will include lead, top rope, auto-belay walls, youth-specific climbing features, and bouldering.
  • Inside, there are climbing areas for everyone. From beginner auto-belay walls to our top roping room, lead climbing pit, and two bouldering areas. The Kid Zone is built for kids, fun for everyone. For the more experienced, there is a training area featuring a campus, moon, and adjustable wall.
