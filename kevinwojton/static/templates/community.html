Community

Providing a Space for Community

Cleveland Rocks is dedicated to providing space for community to develop. Cleveland Rocks will have over 10,000 sqft dedicated to community space. Our goal is to support any local community groups and provide space for future ones. Anything from meet ups, block clubs, local organizations, and/or running clubs will find a home at Cleveland Rocks. Let us know if you have an event or meet up you want to host at our location we will be happy to oblige. We will be leading by example and will be starting a few community groups. These include rock climbing youth team, scholarships to use the center, and technology community groups.
